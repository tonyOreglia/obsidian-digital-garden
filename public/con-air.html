Con Air

  • Rating: 4/5
  • Year: 1997
  • Director: Simon West
  • Language:
  • Actors: Nicholas Cage

This is a true unapologetic action move. It asks the question, can we go a little bit further; and it answers, yes. It's a classic Cage file, so any movie buff must see this as part of film history.

There are a bunch of one liners that seem intended to become memes:

  • Next time, take the bus
  • Happiness, for that gentleman, hurts.
  • Make a move and the bunny gets it.
  • Well, hooray for the sounds of fucking silence!
  • I'm gonna show you that God does exist
