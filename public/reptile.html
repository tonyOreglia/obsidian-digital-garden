Reptile

  • Rating: 3.8/5
  • Year: 2023
  • Director Grant Singer

Not sure I understand the name. The vibe of the movie somehow reminded me of Blade Runner, in terms of the speed and certain details (it was not sci-fi, or visually dark or anything).

Benicio del Toro is always entertaining to watch. I like the movie because it is unique in how it highlights and presents characters and information; this alone I found intriguing. The action and plot unfolds nicely and makes sense. Nothing jaw dropping, but a solid, entertaining move.
