The Upside

  • Rating: 4/5
  • Year: 2017
  • Director: Neil Burger
  • Genre: Drama/Comedy
  • Actos: Bryan Cranston, Kevin Hart, Nicole Kidman

I really enjoyed this film. It is a remake of the French 2011 film The Intouchables; which is an another great movie. With remakes though, you never know. It can be hard to re-capture the same magic; but this film really does it. The dynamic between the characters is really authentic and fun to watch. The acting never seems forced. Actually, I think Kevin Hart really shows how far he's come as a serious actor in this film. The story is great; and actually I didn't remember the details from the original so I was surprised how the story resolved. It a classic tale of someone overcoming their inner struggles. Two unlikely characters helping each other in ways that neither suspects at first. Entertaining, hilarious at times and heartfelt.
