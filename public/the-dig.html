The Dig

  • Rating: 3.1/5
  • Year: 2021
  • Director: Simon Stone
  • Actors: Lily James, Carey Mulligan, Ralph Fiennes, Johnny Flynn
  • genre: Drama/History

Not my cup of tea. But, for someone that very much enjoys period pieces and and subtle drama; it's a quality movie, well made, thoughtful, with good acting. It's really like a history piece of history itself in the making; or unearthing / finding. For me it was a bit slow, the contextual history (WWII) wasn't such that there was a lot to learn there. The drama was actually poignant but somehow underwhelming; maybe, during Covid quarantine, I am just in the mood for a something with more escapism.
