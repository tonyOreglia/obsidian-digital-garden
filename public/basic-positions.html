Basic Positions

https://www.bjjnc.com/most-common-positions-in-brazilian-jiu-jitsu-guard-mount-back-mount-side-control/#:~:text=There%20are%20four%20common%20positions,superior%20part%20of%20your%20game.

Closed Guard ~ between opponents legs, feet locked

Open Guard ~ between opponents legs, feet open

Side Control (Side Mount, Cross Body) ~ Chest to chest

Mount ~ Sitting on torso; one knee on each side

Back Mount ~ If you have your opponents back

Turtle Position ~ Balled up, with elbows and knees tucked in

Knee on Belly ~ knee on partners belly

Half Guard ~ One leg stuck between opponents legs
