Love and Monsters

  • Rating: 4/5
  • Year: 2020
  • Director Michael Matthews
  • Genre:
  • Actors: Dylan O'Brien

This movie is awesome. It's a lot of fun to watch. It kind of has like a Deadpool vibe. It's silly but you still enjoy the tension and action scenes; and it's not so silly that the relationships don't matter. Dylan O'Brien's acting is great and really funny. The CGI is really cool; crazy monsters of all sorts.
