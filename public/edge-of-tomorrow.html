Edge of Tomorrow

  • Rating: 3.9/5
  • Year: 2015
  • Director: Doug Liman
  • Genre: Sci-fi, Action
  • Actors: Tom Cruise, Emily Blunt

Watched this on a flight. I didn't expect it to be that great but it's really a great movie. The acting and chemistry between Cruise and Blunt is believable, funny and you want them together. The plot fits together despite some really tricky storylines; they manage to pull the viewer along without any awkwardness, confusion, or obvious contradictions. The action builds well and the climax is a awesome. the resolution leaves you pretty damn content with the whole thing. I liked the move a lot.
